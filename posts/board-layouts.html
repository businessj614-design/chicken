The Art of the Knockback: A PvP Deep Dive

Keep players engaged with combat that feels fair and impactful.

Published 2025-08-22

Good combat design invites mastery instead of frustration, guiding players to improve with every fight.

Start with Impact

Let newcomers feel the power of a well-timed hit before raising the stakes.

Consistent Physics

Ensure knockback distance and direction are predictable based on the weapon and angle of attack.

Clear Feedback

Show clear cues—animations, sounds, or particles—so success comes from learning rather than guessing.
