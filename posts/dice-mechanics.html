Mastering the Void: A Guide to Bridging

Practical choices that keep your movement fluid and safe.

Published 2025-08-22

Smart building techniques help you cross gaps quickly and stay one step ahead of your opponents.

Speed Bridging

Learn the rhythm of placing blocks while sprinting to cross to the next island in seconds.

Defensive Bridging

Place blocks to the side as you go, creating cover from projectiles while you advance.

Baiting Opponents

Build a weak bridge and wait for an enemy to follow, then break it to send them into the void.
